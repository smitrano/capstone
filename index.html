Etsy

Etsy is a marketplace that fosters human connection by bringing together creative small business owners with buyers around the world looking for unique goods with a personal story.

Etsy’s heart and soul is its global community: creative entrepreneurs who run their businesses in the Etsy marketplace, shoppers looking for things they can’t find anywhere else, manufacturers who partner with Etsy sellers to help them grow, and Etsy employees who build and nurture the marketplace.

Doing good for the world is good for business.

It's not about corporate philanthropy. Sustainable, purpose-driven businesses have advantages in the market that allow them to outperform their more traditional peers.

Access to Capital

There has been a dramatic increase in the number of investors seeking to add sustainability and environmental, social, and governance factors into their portfolio construction. - Goldman Sachs

Goldman Sachs Global Investment Research 2007
The EAI, an asset management group that specializes in sustainable investing, has grown to 27 member institutions, representing US $2.4tn in assets under management.

Improved Performance

High sustainability companies significantly outperform their more traditional counterparts over the long term, both in terms of stock market as well as accounting performance. - Harvard

Harvard Study: The Impact of Corporate Sustainability on Organizational Processes and Performance
Evolution of $1 invested in the stock market in value-weighted portfolios.

Attractive to Talent

When looking at their career goals, today's Millennials are just as interested in how a business develops its people and its contribution to society as they are in its products and profits.
- BARRY SALZBERG, CEO OF DELOITTE GLOBAL

The Deloitte Millennial Survey found that millennials expect business to be good for individuals by offering employment, and to have a positive impact on wider society.

Increased Credibility

A good relationship with NGOs, citizens, and governments is not some vague objective that's nice to achieve if possible. It is a key determinant of competitiveness. - McKinsey

A Weber Shandwick 2012 study revealed that 70% of consumers avoid buying a product if they don’t like the company behind the product.
